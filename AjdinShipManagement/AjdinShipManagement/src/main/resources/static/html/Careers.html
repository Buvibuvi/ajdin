CAREERS

Merchant Navy is the founding stone of international trade, transporting freight/cargo all around the world with most of the import-export business would dependent on it. Both public & private sector shipping companies efficiently operate the Merchant Navy.


In the present day, India supplies around 12.8% of officers and around 14.5% of ratings to the world seafaring community which is one of the highest of any country.


Pave your own way with us for your career on modern ships like cruises, container ships, tankers, ferries or coast guard vessels. Besides the great payscale, the opportunity to climb up the career ladder by engaging in various exciting activities is how you can ensure to make your way to the top.


We wish all the future seafarers great luck for their upcoming endeavors and a fruitful career.
