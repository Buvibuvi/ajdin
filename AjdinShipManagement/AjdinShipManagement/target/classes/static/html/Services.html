SERVICES

Crew Management

Crewing Operations is the key to managing the Shipping operations, right from the beginning of a Ship takeover. The team at Ajdin Ship Management Pvt. Ltd. ensures only the skilled and trained seafarer to join the fleet. ASM follows multinationality crew on board, so as to provide best expertise at an economical rate while adhering to latest the STCW Conventions, as amended and ISM & ISO codes.

Administration

The team at Ajdin Ship Management Pvt. Ltd. provides all administrative aspects of crew requirements including travel plan for joining vessel, visa, repatriation, payment and accounting of wages, overtime and bonus.


All assistance regarding medical tests, fitness certifications and working gear as per safety/environmental protection policy of the Company are provided. The team also ensures all certifications are in place under the flag state / Local / National requirements.

Technical Management

Ajdin Ship Management Pvt. Ltd. is recognized for its high level of technical expertise by its team which comprises of Master Mariners and Chief Engineers of the highest caliber and with decades of experience in their respective field. Ajdin Ship Management Pvt. Ltd. provides full maritime services to all types of vessels.


The team at Ajdin Ship Management Pvt. Ltd. is committed to operate a well maintained vessel with safety of crew, ship and environment as priority. Our technical and marine team is always at the forefront to maintain the vessel in compliance with all regulatory requirements and operate the vessel to highest industry standards. This allows optimal utilization of the vessel by all stakeholders.


Due to the company's outstanding record of professionalism and business practices, we enjoy a highly competitive rate structure from various vendors and suppliers and the benefits accrued are enjoyed by our Principals.
